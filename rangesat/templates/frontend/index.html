RangeSAT: Providing Rangeland Vegetation Monitoring Tools for adaptive Monitoring and Management

Description:

RangeSAT visualizes and graphs estimates of vegetation (biomass, cover, NDVI) at management scales derived from Landsat satellite data. The interface lets users easily map vegetation biomass and cover across their ranch or management area, graph vegetation amounts at multiple times within a year as well as track vegetation amounts across years . This interface also graphs precipitation to help with decision making and interpretation of year to year vegetation fluctuations.

RangeSAT is an ongoing project being developed at the University of Idaho, in partnership with The Nature Conservancy. Feedback, comments and questions can be directed to vjansen@uidaho.edu.
